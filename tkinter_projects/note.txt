There are three types of buttons: 
   • Button 
   • Checkbutton 
   • Radiobutton
There are four types of entry widgets for text or numbers, which allow to 
input text with the keyboard or making a choice uint the mouse:
   • Entry 
   • Combobox 
   • Spinbox 
   • Scale
Then there are the two complex display widgets for text and graphics: 
   • Text 
   • Canvas 
These two widgets present lists:
   • Listbox 
   • Treeview 
Finally these widgets are helper widgets:
   • Frame 
   • Separator
   • Window 
   • Scrollbar 
   • SizeGrip 
   • Progressbar 
   • Menu 
   • ContextMenu
